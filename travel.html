Travel Information

Plan your journey to the villa. Here are some details to make your travel easier.

Nearest Airports

The closest airports are Florence Peretola (FLR) and Pisa Galileo Galilei (PSA). Both are about 1.5 hours away by car.

Car Rentals

We recommend renting a car upon arrival for the flexibility to explore Tuscany. Major rental companies are available at both airports.

Driving Directions

From Florence, take the A1 motorway toward Bologna, exit at Barberino di Mugello, then follow signs to Montalbano. GPS coordinates for Villa Esengrini Montalbano: 44.0078° N, 11.3255° E.

Public Transport

Trains run from Florence to the town of Borgo San Lorenzo. From there, you can take a taxi to the villa (about 15 minutes).
