Getting There

Travel Information

Everything on this page is specific to our wedding weekend in Varese, Italy.

Nearest airport

Milan Malpensa Airport is the closest airport to the venue and is about 25 minutes from Villa Esengrini Montalbano.

Flying from North Carolina

Most guests will likely fly from North Carolina, especially from Charlotte Douglas International Airport.

Recommended trip timing

We suggest arriving on Friday and departing on Monday so you can enjoy the full wedding weekend.

Transportation

Taxis, private cars, and rental cars are all good options for getting around during the weekend.

Passport reminder

Please make sure your passport is valid well in advance of the trip.
