Travel Information

Plan your journey to the villa. Here are some details to make your travel easier.

Nearest Airport

The nearest airport is Milan Malpensa (MXP), about 25 minutes by car from Villa Esengrini Montalbano in Varese.

Travel From North Carolina

Most of our guests will be flying from North Carolina, likely via Charlotte Douglas International Airport. We recommend arriving on Friday and departing on Monday to enjoy the full weekend.

Transportation Options

Taxis, private car services, and rental cars are all available at Milan Malpensa. Choose whichever option best fits your plans and group size.

Passport Reminder

Please ensure that your passport is valid well in advance of the trip to Italy.
