Weekend Timeline

Below is our tentative schedule for the wedding weekend. Times are approximate and will be confirmed closer to the date.

Friday
Guest Arrivals & Welcome

Guests arrive and check in. We'll organise an optional welcome dinner or informal gathering – exact details to follow.

Saturday
Wine Tour & Dinner

Enjoy a wine bus tour with our guests. Afterwards we'll have a group dinner together or give you time to explore on your own.

Sunday
Wedding Day

Bride preparations begin around 12:00 PM. Guests arrive around 4:30 PM and the ceremony will start at approximately 5:00 PM. Afterward there will be an aperitivo, dinner in the Scuderia, then cake and dancing. Our evening will conclude at midnight, with the possibility of extra time.

Monday
Farewell & Departures

Join us for an optional brunch before departures.
