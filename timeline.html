Wedding Weekend

Weekend Timeline

Times are approximate and will be confirmed closer to the wedding.

Friday

Arrivals and check in

Guests arrive, settle in, and enjoy the start of the weekend.

An optional welcome dinner or informal gathering is planned. Final details will be shared later.

Saturday

Wine bus tour

We plan to host a winery bus trip for guests during the day.

Afterward, we will either gather for dinner or leave the evening open for everyone to enjoy Varese at their own pace.

Sunday

Wedding day at Villa Esengrini Montalbano

Bride prep begins around 12:00 PM.

Guest arrival is expected around 4:30 PM, with the ceremony beginning around 5:00 PM.

Aperitivo will follow the ceremony, then dinner in the Scuderia, followed by cake and dancing.

The evening is expected to end at midnight, with possible extra time if finalized.

Monday

Optional brunch and departures

We will likely have an optional brunch before departures.

We are also considering a Lake Como outing with Villa del Balbianello for guests staying on.
